Welcome

Hi! 👋 This is the EBHCS Bulletin Board.

Your advisors at East Boston Harborside post things here for you — jobs, ESOL classes, college info, immigration help, food and housing, events, and more.

This guide takes 2 minutes. Tap Next to start.

The two big ideas

Bulletins = the live feed

When an advisor — Leah, Jerome, Carmen, Mike, anyone — posts something new, it shows up on the home screen. The newest stuff is at the top.

The two big ideas

Resources = always there

Need help with housing, food, immigration, ESOL, college & GED, money, or health? Tap Find Help. These links stay there all the time — you can come back any day.

[ Capture as: onboarding-photos/find-help.png — Find Help page with category cards ]
Other useful tabs

Calendar and About

  • Calendar — see what's coming up at EBHCS (events, deadlines, school breaks).
  • About — office hours, the school address, and your advisors' names and emails.
[ Capture as: onboarding-photos/bottom-nav.png — Bottom nav bar with Feed, Calendar, Find Help, About ]
Need to talk to an advisor?

Here's how to reach them.

  1. Open the About page.
  2. Tap See your advisor to open the list.
  3. Find your advisor, then tap the ✉️ email icon next to their name to send a message.
[ Capture as: onboarding-photos/advisor-list.png — About page with the advisor list expanded ]
💡 You can also stop by during office hours — they're on the same About page, right above the advisor list.
In your language

You can switch to Spanish anytime.

Tap the EN / ES button at the top to change the language. Puedes cambiar al español cuando quieras.

Put it on your phone

iPhone — Add to Home Screen

  1. Open the bulletin board in Safari.
  2. Tap the Share button (the square with an up arrow).
  3. Scroll and tap Add to Home Screen.
  4. Tap Add.
[ Capture as: onboarding-photos/iphone-install.png — iPhone Safari Share menu with "Add to Home Screen" highlighted ]
Put it on your phone

Android — Install the app

  1. Open the bulletin board in Chrome.
  2. Tap the ⋮ menu (three dots, top right).
  3. Tap Add to Home screen or Install app.
  4. Tap Install.
[ Capture as: onboarding-photos/android-install.png — Android Chrome menu with "Install app" highlighted ]
You did it

Now it lives on your phone. 🎉

Tap the icon on your home screen any time. New bulletins will be waiting for you.

💡 Tip: Check it once a day — that's how you'll catch the good stuff first.
Quick recap

Remember these three things:

  • Bulletins = the live feed of new posts.
  • Find Help = the resources that are always there.
  • Add to Home Screen = open it like an app.

That's it. You're ready.
